How It Works

Our Process

A clear, consistent approach from first call to final walkthrough — so you always know where things stand.

01

Consultation

We discuss your project goals, timeline, and what matters most to you — by phone, message, or in person.

02

Site Visit

We visit the property to assess the scope, take measurements, and ask the right questions before quoting.

03

Transparent Estimate

You receive a written estimate with a clear breakdown of materials, labour, and timeline — no guesswork.

04

Project Scheduling

Once approved, we set a start date, confirm materials, and give you a realistic project schedule.

05

Build & Communication

The work is done efficiently, with regular updates and a clean, organized job site throughout.

06

Final Walkthrough

We walk through the finished project together — making sure everything meets your expectations before we close out.
